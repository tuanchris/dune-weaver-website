## What is a Kinetic Sand Table?

A kinetic sand table is a captivating device that uses a magnet-driven ball bearing to create intricate patterns in sand. Controlled by precision motors and creative software, these tables produce ever-changing geometric designs that are both relaxing and inspiring to watch.

## Our Mission

Our goal is to make kinetic sand tables accessible to everyone. Whether you're a seasoned maker or just getting started, we provide:

- **Open-source designs** that you can build yourself
