# Getting Started with Dune Weaver

Welcome to Dune Weaver! This guide will help you get started with your kinetic sand table.

## What You'll Need

- Your Dune Weaver unit
- Power supply (12V DC)
- Fine sand (recommended: white play sand or specialty kinetic sand)
- USB cable for configuration (optional)

## Initial Setup

### 1. Unboxing and Inspection

Carefully unbox your Dune Weaver and inspect all components:
- Main table unit
- Power supply
- Magnetic ball bearing
- Setup tools

### 2. Placing Your Table

Choose a stable, level surface for your Dune Weaver. The table should be:
- Away from direct sunlight
- On a vibration-free surface
- Accessible for maintenance

### 3. Adding Sand

1. Pour sand slowly into the table bed
2. Distribute evenly across the surface
3. Use approximately 2-3 pounds of sand for optimal performance
4. Gently level the surface

### 4. First Power-On

1. Connect the power supply to the table
2. Plug into a wall outlet
3. The table will perform a homing sequence
4. Your first pattern will begin automatically!

## Troubleshooting

If you encounter issues during setup:
- Ensure power supply is properly connected
- Check that the magnetic ball moves freely
- Verify sand depth is appropriate (not too deep or shallow)

For more help, visit our [troubleshooting guide](/docs/troubleshooting).
